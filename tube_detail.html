A remarkable number of technological uses have been suggested for nanotubes. Most of these are in the field of molecular-scale computing or other topics of nanotechnology.

  • The BBC Online news website frequently carries stories about nanotubes. This search might be a good start.
  • A longer article from American Scientist magazine: "Fullerene Nanotubes: C1,000,000 and Beyond", by Boris Yakobson and Richard E. Smalley.

The mathematics behind and implementation of the tubetype generator are a collaboration of Gunnar Brinkmann, Ulrike v. Nathusius and Adam Palser. A scientific reference for this generator is:

  • A constructive enumeration of nanotube caps, Discrete Applied Mathematics, Volume 116, Issues 1-2, 15 January 2002, Pages 55-71, G. Brinkmann, U. v. Nathusius and A. H. R. Palser.
